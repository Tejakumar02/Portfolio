About
Philosophy

My work focuses on bridging the gap between object detection accuracy and real-world reliability. In industrial CV, high mAP alone is insufficient — systems must reason about position, sequence, and time.

I specialize in fixed-camera vision systems where spatial validation, temporal smoothing, and rule-based state tracking transform frame-level detections into dependable operational signals.

System Constraints I Work Under
  • Fixed camera placement · constant field-of-view
  • Offline-only inference — no cloud access
  • Latency-sensitive pipeline under continuous operation
  • No model retraining allowed post-deployment
  • Real-world noise: motion blur, hand occlusion, lighting shifts

How I Build Systems

From raw problem to production — my end-to-end workflow.

👁
01 Problem Definition Understand the industrial process — what needs to be detected, where the camera is fixed, and what counts as a valid vs. invalid state.
📸
02 Data Curation Collect real production footage. Curate, clean, and label using CVAT — 17K+ images, 90K+ annotations covering edge cases and failure modes.
🧠
03 Model Training Train YOLO-based models with augmentation pipelines. Validate against production metrics — mAP50, precision, recall — not just benchmark numbers.
📐
04 Spatial & Temporal Logic Layer centroid-based ROI validation and state-machine tracking on top of detections. Suppresses false positives from motion blur and occlusion.
🚀
05 Deploy & Monitor Flask REST API + live monitoring dashboard. Offline-only, air-gapped. Continuous performance tracking with known-failure documentation.
